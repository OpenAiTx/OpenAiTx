## Introduction

HelloGitHub shares interesting and beginner-friendly open source projects on GitHub. **Every month on the 28th**, we [publish updates](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/mp/appmsgalbum?__biz=MzA5MzYyNzQ0MQ==&action=getalbum&album_id=1331197538447310849#wechat_redirect) in the form of a monthly publication. The content includes **interesting, beginner-level open source projects**, **open source books**, **practical projects**, **enterprise-level projects**, and more. Experience the charm of open source in a short time and fall in love with it!
