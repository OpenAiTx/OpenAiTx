## Overview

**Problem** 

Developers waste time repeatedly explaining context to AI tools. Hit token limits in Claude? You have to start over and lose everything. Switch from ChatGPT/Claude to Cursor? You must explain your context again. Your conversations, decisions, and insights disappear between sessions.

**Solution** - **CORE** (**Contextual Observation & Recall Engine**)

CORE is an open-source, unified, persistent memory layer for all your AI tools. Your context follows you from Cursor to Claude to ChatGPT to Claude Code. A single knowledge graph tracks who said what, when, and why. Connect once, remember everywhere. Stop managing context and start building.

## üöÄ Get Started
**Build your unified memory graph in 5 minutes:**

1. **Sign Up** at [core.heysol.ai](https://core.heysol.ai) and create your account
2. **Add your first memory** - share context about yourself

5. **Test it out** - ask "What do you know about me?" in the conversation section
